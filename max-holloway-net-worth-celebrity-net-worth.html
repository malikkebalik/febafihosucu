What is Max Holloway's Net Worth?

Max Holloway is an American mixed martial artist who has a net worth of $2 million. Max Holloway was born in Honolulu, Hawaii in December 1991. He has competed as a featherweight and lightweight. Holloway made his professional MMA debut in September 2010 with a win over Duke Saragosa. In March 2011 he won the X-1 Lightweight Championship by beating Harris Sarmineto. Max Holloway made his UFC debut in July 2011 with a win over Eddie Rincon.

In January 2014 he had the Knockout of the Night over Will Chope. Holloway had the Performance of the Night in his wins over Akira Corassani in October 2014 and Cub Swanson in April 2015. In December 2016 he won the interim UFC Featherweight Championship and had the Performance of the Night with a win over Anthony Pettis. Max Holloway won and unified the UFC Featherweight Championship and had the Fight of the Night with a win over Jose Aldo in June 2017. He had the Performance of the Night and the Fight of the Night in December 2018 defeating Brian Ortega.
