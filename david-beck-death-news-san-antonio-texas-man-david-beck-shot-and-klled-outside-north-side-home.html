David Beck, a citizen of San Antonio, Texas, reportedly died as a result of wounds he received during the shooting incident.

According to San Antonio police, the incident happened just after 8:30 a.m. on the 1000 block of W. Huisache Avenue, close to Blanco Road. The landlord questioned a man about some persistent difficulties, and that’s how the confrontation started.

Both of them drew guns and got into a furious quarrel during their fight. The landlord is said to have shot the man several times, hitting him in the head, when the situation got out of hand.

The individual who had been hurt was then taken to the hospital and eventually declared deceased. To find out more about the incident’s circumstances, the authorities have launched an inquiry and questioned the landlord.

Determining whether the shooting was a self-defense tactic is the main focus. It’s still unclear what exactly went wrong that caused the incident. Thankfully, there were no further reported injuries, and the police were able to retrieve both of the firearms used in the incident.
