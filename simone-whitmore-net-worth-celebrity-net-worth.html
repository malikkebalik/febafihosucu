What is Simone Whitmore's Net Worth?

Simone Whitmore is an American doctor and reality television star who has a net worth of $3.5 million. Simone Whitmore is an Atlanta-based OBGYN who operates her own practice. Whitmore is one of the stars of Bravo's latest reality show Married to Medicine. The show follows six glamorous women in Atlanta who are either doctors themselves or married to doctors. Simone Whitmore is one of the cast members who is actually a doctor. Whitmore is originally from Nashville, Tennessee. She attended Spelman College for her pre-med undergraduate degree, and after Spelman, Simone attended medical school at East Tennessee State University. She then moved to Boston Medical Center for residency. Simone is now a solo-practitioner and owns the North Perimeter OBGYN. Simone has been married to Cecil Whitmore for 16 years and the couple has two sons, Miles and Michael. Her husband is considerably more laid back than Simone. Her staff calls her "the Warden" and "Witchmore" in jest, and she always presses the line of hard work, never settling for mediocrity. Today Simone Whitmore is known as a stern boss and active supporter of her alma maters. She is a member of the American College of OBGYN and the American Board of OBGYN.
