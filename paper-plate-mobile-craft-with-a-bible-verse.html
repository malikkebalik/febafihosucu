Paper Plate Mobile Craft with a Bible Verse

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, September 25, 2024

Paper Plate Mobile Craft with Bible Verse

This paper plate mobile craft is a fun and creative way to display verses about love (like those found in 1 Corinthians 13) for Valentine’s Day.

Supplies:

2 Paper Plates
Decorating Pens
Construction Paper
String

Directions:

1. Cut out the inside circle of the paper plates.

2. Decorate both inside and outside of each place, and allow to dry.

3. Cut 2 heart shapes out of construction paper. Write a verse on each heart like “love is patient” and “love is kind.”

4. Glue a string between the back side of the hearts.

5. Run the string through the paper plates, and glue them together. Hang in a prominent place.
