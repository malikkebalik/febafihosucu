Who is Stephanie Hsu? 

Stephanie Hsu is an American actress known for her roles in movies, TV shows, and Broadway musicals. 

She was born in 1990 in California to Taiwanese parents. Hsu graduated from NYU and started her career by acting in experimental theater and comedy shows. 

Her notable works include The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel, Be More Chill musical, and the hit movie Everything Everywhere All at Once, for which she received an Oscar nomination. 

With her talent and versatility in comedy and drama roles, Stephanie Hsu has become one of the rising stars in Hollywood.

Early Life of Stephanie Hsu 

Stephanie Hsu was born on 25 November 1990 in Los Angeles, California. She came from a Chinese immigrant family and was raised alongside her siblings in LA. 

Stephanie developed an interest in acting from a young age. After finishing high school, she studied at New York University’s Tisch School of Arts. 

There, she majored in drama and trained with the Atlantic Theater Company. Stephanie studied hard to improve her acting skills. 

She also interned in Amsterdam during her college years to further her learning.

Stephanie came from a supportive family who encouraged her passion. However, as one of the few Asian students in her acting class, she sometimes felt like an outsider. 

This gave her extra motivation to prove herself in the entertainment industry. Overall, her education helped Stephanie develop a strong foundation that prepared her for an acting career.

Stephanie Hsu Career 

Stephanie Hsu has had an impressive career in both theater and television. She started her career on Broadway, starring in SpongeBob SquarePants and Be More Chill productions. This helped build her credentials in the entertainment industry.

She then began landing roles on television shows. She had a recurring role on the popular sitcom The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel starting in 2019. 

Hsu also appeared in shows like Girl Code, Nora from Queens, and Awkwafina Is Nora from Queens. These roles on television exposed her talent to wider audiences.

While working consistently in theater and television, Hsu got her breakout role in 2022. She played the complex roles of Joy and Jobu in the acclaimed film Everything Everywhere All At Once. 

Her nuanced and emotional performance earned her widespread acclaim and an Oscar nomination for Best Supporting Actress. This affirmed Hsu as a talented actress capable of tackling diverse roles.

With her career advancing, Stephanie Hsu has proven herself a skilled performer across film, television, and theater. 

Her commitment to her craft and compelling on-screen presence should lead to many more prominent roles in the future.
