Episode 75: Barstool Reags On If Kentucky Can Win It All + Champions Classic Recap

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, August 15, 2024

Subscribe to Mostly Sports with Mark Titus and Brandon Walker on Youtube: https://www.youtube.com/@MostlySportsTitusandWalker?sub_confirmation=1 

On today's episode we recap the Champions Classic, and then we are joined by Barstool's Reags to talk Kentucky Basketball. On the back half of the episode Mark and TJ talk Ed Cooley throwing a fit after losing to Rutgers, Michigan looking good, Wisconsin looking bad, the Maui Invitational, and more. 

Reags is a blogger and a content creator covering college hoops, NBA Draft, NBA and other shenanigans at Barstool Sports.
