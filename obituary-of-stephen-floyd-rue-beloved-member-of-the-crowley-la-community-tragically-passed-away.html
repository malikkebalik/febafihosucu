Obituary of Stephen Floyd Rue: Beloved Member of the Crowley, LA Community Tragically Passed Away

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, September 28, 2024

The passing of Stephen Floyd Rue has left a profound impact on the community of Crowley. Known for his kindness, generosity, and dedication to his family, Stephen peacefully passed away at the age of 70, surrounded by loved ones. A funeral ceremony will be held on November 29, 2023, at the Crowley chapel of Geesey-Ferguson Funeral Home, with Rev. Jason Corder and Rev. David Broussard presiding. Visiting hours will be from 9:00 am until the start of the service at 1:00 pm, followed by Stephen’s burial in Maxie Cemetery. Stephen is survived by his loving wife, four children, siblings, and cherished grandchildren. In lieu of flowers, the family requests memorial contributions to be made to St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital. Let us come together as a community to honor the memory of Stephen Floyd Rue, a remarkable individual who made a difference in the world. Condolences and messages of sympathy can be shared with the family through www.geesey-ferguson.com. Geesey-Ferguson Funeral Home of Crowley will handle the arrangements, ensuring a dignified tribute to this beloved community figure.

Obituary of Stephen Floyd Rue – A Beloved Community Figure Remembered

With heavy hearts, we share the news of the passing of Stephen Floyd Rue, a beloved member of the Crowley community. Stephen was known for his kind heart, generous spirit, and unwavering dedication to his family. On November 25, 2023, at the age of 70, Stephen peacefully left this world, surrounded by his loved ones. His departure has left a void in the hearts of those who had the privilege of knowing him.

Remembering Stephen Floyd Rue

Stephen Floyd Rue will forever be remembered for his caring nature and the positive impact he had on the lives of many. His legacy of kindness and compassion will continue to inspire others. Stephen’s loving wife of 35 years, Priscilla T. Rue, and their four children – Floyd and Sara Rue, Samuel and Rebecca Rue, Amy and Daniel Mouton, and Valarie and Garrett Myers – carry on his cherished memory. Alongside his immediate family, Stephen leaves behind his siblings, Byron and Sherry Rue, Johnny and JoAnn Rue, Virginia R. Dunnahoe and her husband Duane, as well as his nine adored grandchildren.

Family and Legacy

Stephen Floyd Rue’s family extended beyond his immediate household, encompassing siblings, in-laws, and cherished grandchildren. His siblings, Byron and Sherry Rue, Johnny and JoAnn Rue, and Virginia R. Dunnahoe and her husband Duane, were not only his family but also his closest friends. Their bond was forged through shared experiences, laughter, and unwavering support.

Stephen’s nine cherished grandchildren brought immense joy and happiness to his life. Their laughter and innocence filled his heart with love and pride. He cherished every moment spent with them, creating lasting memories that will be treasured by the entire family.

Stephen’s family was a source of strength and comfort to him throughout his life. Their unwavering support and love provided him with the foundation to make a difference in the world and leave a lasting legacy.

Funeral Arrangements

The funeral service for Stephen Floyd Rue will be a time to honor and remember his remarkable life. It will be held on November 29, 2023, at the Crowley chapel of the Geesey-Ferguson Funeral Home. This solemn occasion will provide an opportunity for family, friends, and community members to come together and pay their respects to Stephen.

Visitation and Funeral Service

Visitation hours will begin at 9:00 am, allowing attendees to gather and share their memories of Stephen before the funeral service commences at 1:00 pm. Reverend Jason Corder and Reverend David Broussard, esteemed members of the community, will preside over the ceremony. They will offer words of comfort, remembrance, and celebration of Stephen’s life, highlighting the impact he had on the community and the lives of those around him.

Burial and Final Resting Place

Following the funeral service, Stephen Floyd Rue will be laid to rest in the Maxie Cemetery. This serene and peaceful location will serve as his final resting place, where he will find eternal peace. The cemetery will be a place where family and friends can visit to pay their respects, reflect on Stephen’s life, and find solace in the memories they shared with him.

Memorial Contributions and Condolences

In honor of Stephen Floyd Rue’s memory and his commitment to making a difference, the family kindly requests that memorial contributions be made to St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital. By supporting this esteemed institution, we can continue Stephen’s mission of bringing hope and healing to children in need. Your contribution will make a meaningful impact on the lives of young patients and their families, ensuring that Stephen’s legacy of compassion and generosity lives on.

Memorial Contributions to St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital
