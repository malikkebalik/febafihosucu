Is Mel Tucker Still Married to His Wife Jo-Ellyn Tucker?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, July 23, 2024

American football coach Mel Tucker is well-known for coaching teams in the college game. He was born in Cleveland, Ohio, on January 4, 1972 and played defensive back for the Wisconsin Badgers during a successful playing career before switching to coaching.

Fans have been wondering if he’s yet married or not and in this post, you’ll know the answer.

Is Mel Tucker Married?

Yes, the head football coach at Michigan State has been wed to Jo-Ellyn Tucker for more than 20 years. When he was the defensive backs coach at LSU in 2000, she got married to the Michigan State coach.

You can see the beautiful bond of the couple in an Instagram post shared by Mel on May 7, 2018 with a caption, “DerbyDay with the most Beautifullest Girl in the world @joellyn_tucker.”

She allegedly met Tucker on a lucky blind date in the late 1990s. A common classmate who knew Tucker from his time at the University of Wisconsin set up their meeting when Jo-Ellyn was a law student at Rutgers University at the time.

Who is Jo-Ellyn Tucker?

Jo-Ellyn is a former lawyer who has a strong interest in stewardship, nonprofit volunteer work and leadership in organizations. Following her time as a practising attorney, Jo-Ellyn is said to have turned her attention to charitable work and community service.

Jo-Ellyn was raised and was born in Lansing, Michigan and after graduating from high school there, she continued her studies at Rutgers. She graduated from Rutgers Law School with a Juris Doctor and has a B.A. in English from the University of Illinois.

Her dedication extends beyond her family; she actively participates in several philanthropic projects and is constantly striving to make a positive difference in society.

Her charity activities cover a range of positions and duties including those of founding partner and board president of the Investment Club, Big Brothers Big Sisters of the Capital Region and the Capital Area Humane Society.
