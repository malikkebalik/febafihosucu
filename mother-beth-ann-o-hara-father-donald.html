Meet Sarah Silverman Parents: Mother Beth Ann O’Hara And Father Donald Silverman

Sarah was born in Bedford, New Hampshire, on December 1, 1970. Sarah Silverman’s parents are Beth Ann O’Hara and Donald Silverman. Her Father, Donald, died on 11 May 2023.

She had a mom who took pictures for George McGovern and a dad who owned a store called Crazy Sophie’s Outlet and helped people.

Sarah and her three sisters grew up in a Jewish home in Manchester, New Hampshire. When she was a kid, her mom and dad split up. Later, her mom married a man named John O’Hara. Sarah thinks he’s a good stepdad.

Sarah’s mom died in 2015, which made the family feel sad and empty. Sarah talked about her mom’s struggle with sadness and how it affected her family. She talked about mental health problems and supported getting help for them using her platform.

Sarah liked her childhood and what her parents taught her, even though they separated and her mother had a hard time. She learned about empathy from her mother and social justice from her father.

Sarah is still doing comedy, acting, and writing to keep her parents’ memory alive. She helped causes that her parents care about, like mental health and social justice, with her success.
