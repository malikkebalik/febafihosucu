What is Pollyanna Chu's Net Worth?

Pollyanna Chu is a Hong Kong businesswoman who has a net worth of $1.5 billion. One of the most powerful businesswomen in her country, Pollyanna Chu serves as CEO of Kingston Securities. She co-founded the company with her husband Nicolas, who serves as the company's chairman.

Aside from Kingston Securities, she has been investing in real estate holdings in Beijing since 1992. Her holdings there only top off her wealth. A mother of two, she named her son Kingston and he now runs the family-controlled, Hong Kong-listed Sincere Watch.
