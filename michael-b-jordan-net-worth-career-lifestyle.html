Michael B. Jordan Net Worth: Career &amp; Lifestyle

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, September 19, 2024

Michael B. Jordan Net Worth is $25 million as an American actor and producer.

Best known for his roles as shooting victim Oscar Grant in the 2013 drama “Fruitvale Station,” boxer Donnie Creed in the 2015 movie”Creed,” and Erik Killmonger in the 2018 MCU movie “Black Panther.”

All of these three films were written and directed by Ryan Coogler.

Michael was named one of the 100 most influential people in the world by Time magazine in 2020.

The same year The New York Times named ranked him number 15 on its list of the 25 Greatest Actors of the 21st Century.

Similarly, Michael was named the Sexiest Man Alive by People magazine.

Apart from that, Michael reprised his boxer role in 2018 “Creed II” and is set to make his directorial debut in 2022 “Creed III.”

Before landing on the big screen, Michael broke out in television roles, including Wallace on the 2002 HBO crime drama series “The Wire,” Reggie Montgomery on the 2003-2006 ABC soap opera “All My Children, “and Vince Howard in the 2009-2011 NBC sports drama series “Friday Night Lights.”

Michael B. Jordan: Early Life

Michael Jordan was born Michael Bakari Jordan on 9th February 1987 in Santa Ana, California, United States.

His father is Michael A. Jordan, and his mother is Donna Jordan, who worked in Newark Arts High School.

He grew up with his two siblings younger brother Khalid Jordan who signed to be a football player at Howard University in 2010, and his younger Jamila Jordan.

His family moved to Newark, New Jersey, after spending two years in California.

Moreover, Michael attended Newark Arts High School, where he played basketball, and it’s the same school where his mother worked.

Before embarking on a full-fledged acting career, Michael worked as a model for several companies and brands such as Toys “R” Us and Modell’s sporting goods.

Following that, he appeared briefly in single episodes of the television series The Sopranos and Cosby, which also kick-started his acting career in 1999.

His big break in the movie industry was Keanu Reeves, starrer 2001 film Hardball.

Similarly, in 2002 he appeared in a small but pivotal role on the first season of HBO’s The Wire as Wallace.

Cars

Michael B. Jordan has a fleet of luxurious cars. His collection has some of the finest cars ever produced, which are super-fast yet practical.

Here are some of the cars Michael B. Jordan owns:

Audi R8

Michael B. Jordan owns a super stylish Audi R8. In fact, he was featured in the 2013 GQ cover with his White Audi R8.

However, it is one of the oldest cars on his fleet. Audi indeed altered and made minor changes for this particular 2013 R8 model.

There were several options to choose from engines wise such as 4.3 liters V8 or 5.3 liters V10. Nonetheless, the car never failed to produce 430 brake horsepower with the V8 variant and 525 brake horsepower with the V10 variant.

Similarly, Audi also introduced its first S-Tronic seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission in 2013.

Likewise, the 2013 model came with LED for head and taillights with some modern touches. It was released at Frankfurt Auto Show, and R8 had the new technology for the time with reimagined blinkers and lightning tricks.

The price for this model in 2013 was more than $160,000.

This one is special because it is Michael’s best car. In fact, it was his favorite car as a child.

It also has an All-Wheel Drive system and nine-speed dual-clutch transmission. Similarly, the car can hit 0-60 miles an hour in less than 3 seconds and has a top speed of 191 miles an hour.

Similarly, it averages 21 MPG in the city and 22 MPG on the highway.

Likewise, the interior features comfortable seating and the best infotainment system.

You can choose your driving options from the infotainment system, such as Sports Mode and Quiet Mode.

Cadillac Escalade

Escalade is the pinnacle of luxurious SUVs with its increasing popularity among celebrities.

Under the hood of this full-size SUV is a V8 engine connected with ten-speed automatic transmission, which pumps about 420 brake horsepower.

The SUV has a towing capacity of 8,300 lbs. What good is a vehicle if the mileage breaks your bank? So with that in mind, people at General Motors developed the engine that did 14 MPG in the city and 21 MPG on the highway despite its ginormous size and V8 engine.

It does not only look fabulous on the outside. The interior features heated seats, a power-adjustable steering wheel, and comfy seats wrapped in the finest leather.

Also infotainment system comes top of the line.

Starting price for this massive SUV is about $76,500.

Hellraiser: Dodge Challenger SRT Hellcat

This might be one of the coolest in the collection of Michael B. Jordan. The power-packed 2019 Dodge Challenger SRT Hellcat produces 717 or 797 brake horsepower, respectively, with an option between an eight-speed automatic and a six-speed automatic.
