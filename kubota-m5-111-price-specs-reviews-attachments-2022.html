Kubota M5-111 Utility Tractor is equipped with a Kubota V3800CR-TIEF4 turbocharged inter-cooled diesel 4-cylinder 16-valve liquid-cooled inline engine, which delivers 105.6 hp (78.7 kW) power at rated rpm 2400.

Kubota M5-111 utility Tractor featured a Kubota 3.8L 4-cyl diesel engine, 4×2 2WD/4×4 MFWD 4WD, power steering, 5181 lbs Rear lift (at 24″/610mm), 11.3 qts 10.7 L engine oil capacity, 8 forward and reverse gears.

Kubota M5-111 Compact Utility tractor attachments include a Kubota LA1854 front-end loader.
