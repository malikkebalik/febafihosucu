Kevin Costner &amp; Christine Baumgartner Split up After Nearly 18 Years of Marriage

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, July 5, 2024

After 18 years of marriage, Kevin Costner’s wife filed for divorce on Monday, May 1, 2023, citing irreconcilable differences as the cause of the split. Arnold Robinson, Costner’s spokesperson stated in a statement:

“It is with great sadness that circumstances beyond his control have transpired which have resulted in Mr. Costner having to participate in a dissolution of marriage.”

Baumgartner placed their separation date as April 11 in her paperwork and asked for shared custody of their three kids, Cayden, Hayes and Grace.

Costner also requested joint custody and cited irreconcilable differences in his statement which was submitted on Tuesday, May 2, 2023. In addition, a prenup regarding spousal support, legal bills and property is mentioned in the actor/director’s file.

Divorce was not Costner’s idea, a source told: “If he could change the situation, he would. It was not anything that he wanted or sought.”

His legaI team listed the following as separate property: Miscellaneous jewelry and other personal effects; Respondent’s earnings and accumulations before marriage, during the marriage and from and after the date of separation and there are additional separate property assets and obligations of each party as mandated by the parties’ Premarital Agreement.

They also added Pursuant to the terms in Paragraph 9.A. of the parties’ Premarital Agreement, Petitioner to vacate Respondent’s separate property residences.

Costner was previously married to Cindy Silva for 16 years before their divorce in 1994. It is said that Costner paid his ex-wife an estimated $80 million in settlement.
