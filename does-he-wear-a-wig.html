Jeffree Star Bald And Cancer Rumors

There have been rumors circulating online that Jeffree Star is bald and has Cancer.

However, these rumors lack substantiation, and no credible evidence supports them.

He has also mentioned having a genetic condition known as androgenetic alopecia, which can result in hair loss in both men and women.

Despite speculation from some individuals that Star’s hair loss may be linked to Cancer or chemotherapy, there is no concrete evidence to support these claims. 

Jeffree has not publicly disclosed any diagnosis of Cancer or having undergone chemotherapy.

However, it is known that Daniel Lucas Oxford, Jeffree Star’s best friend, passed away on November 27, 2022, at 35.
