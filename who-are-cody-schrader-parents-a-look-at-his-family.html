Cody Schrader is an accomplished American college football player known for his role as a running back. He has made significant strides in his football career, particularly during his time with the University of Missouri.

What Role Do His Parents Play in His Career?

Tommy Schrader initially had high hopes for Cody in baseball. However, upon recognizing his son’s potential in football during eighth grade, Tommy shifted his focus to help Cody train in football.

This decision proved pivotal as Cody went on to become one of the most promising running backs in college football. His father’s guidance and training played a significant role in this transition and success.

Cody’s mother, Jenny, contributed significantly to his athletic development, particularly in terms of nutrition. Her support in ensuring Cody received the necessary nutrition to excel in college football is a testament to the holistic support system he had from his family.

Cody often credits his mother for her role in his success, highlighting the importance of a supportive family environment in nurturing athletic talent.

Cody Schrader’s Stats In 2023

With the Missouri Tigers in 2023, Cody Schrader is enjoying a fantastic CFB season. 11 touchdowns and 1,124 yards of rushing have been scored by the running back on 197 carries.

Interestingly, in his last seven games for Missouri, he has scored a touchdown through the air.

The Tigers’ remarkable performances under Schrader have propelled them to second place in the SEC East. With an 8-2 record right now, Missouri is only one game behind the Georgia Bulldogs in the division.

In Week 12, the Tigers will play the Florida Gators on Saturday, Nov. 18. Schrader will be looking to build on his recent success.
