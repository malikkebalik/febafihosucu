Robert Hoffman Net Worth

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, September 1, 2024

Robert Hoffman:

$3 million

Robert Hoffman is an American actor and dancer. His career started in the early 2000s. During his early years, he made guest appearances in Quintuplets and American Dreams. He also appeared as a dancer in a few films with his first credited acting role in the 2004 movie You Got Served. He later appeared as Justin Drayton in the movie She’s the Man. Subsequently, he had roles in the movies Shrooms, National Lampoon’s Bag Boy, and played Chase Collins in Step Up 2: The Streets. Other film credits include Aliens in the Attic, Burning Palms, Take Me Home Tonight, Lap Dance, and Amateur Night.
