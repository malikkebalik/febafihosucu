What is Eddie Guerrero's Net Worth?

Eddie Guerrero was a Mexican American professional wrestler who had a net worth of $6 million. Eddie Guerrero was born in El Paso, Texas in October 1967 and passed away in November 2005. He was a member of the famed Guerrero wrestling family. Guerrero competed in Mexico and Japan as well as for World Wrestling Entertainment, World Championship Wrestling, and Extreme Championship Wrestling. He started out in CMLL in Mexico from 1987 to 1992 and then competed in AAA from 1992 to 1994. Guerrero competed in New Japan Pro Wrestling from 1993 to 1996.

He wrestled for ECW in 1995 and WCW from 1989 to 2000. Guerrero competed in the WWF/WWE from 2000 to his passing in 2005. He held several titles including the WWE Championship, WWE United States Championship, WWE Tag Team Championship four times, WWE Intercontinental Championship two times WWF European Championship two times, WCW United States Heavyweight Championship, WCW Cruiserweight Championship two times, and ECW World Television Championship two times. Guerrero was inducted into the WWE Hall of Fame in 2006. Eddie Guerrero passed away on November 13, 2005 at 38 years old from heart failure.
