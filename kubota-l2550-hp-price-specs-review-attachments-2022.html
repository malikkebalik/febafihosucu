Kubota L2550 HP, Price, Specs, Review, Attachments 2022

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, August 23, 2024

Kubota L2550 Price, Reviews, Specs, Engine Oil Capacity, Weight, Serial Numbers, Attachments, History, Features & Pictures

Kubota L2550 Compact Utility Tractor is equipped with a Kubota D1402-DI-A diesel 3-cylinder liquid-cooled 85.1 ci [1.4 L] engine, which delivers 29.5 hp [22.0 kW] power at rated rpm 2600.

Kubota L2550 Compact Utility Tractor featured a Kubota 1.4L 3-cyl diesel engine, 4×2 2WD or 4×4 MFWD 4WD, differential wet disc brakes, 8 forward and 7 reverse gears, and 32.8 qts [31.0 L] oil capacity.

Kubota L2550 Compact Utility tractor attachments include a mowing deck, front-end loader, and backhoe.
